# Prompt Engineering
fewer tokens used - better output (high information density)
"Dead zone" / Plan A derailed / Don't change your mind (context will pollute)
Write optimal jira ticket - no assumptions, be unambiguous and specific, provide example
tone spartan / caveman / researcher / industry terms / medium polite / third person
Seek the Goldilocks zone of brief and direct while applying relevant constraints and examples. 
give an out if unknown, ask for sources and level of confidence
no leading tones, present equal comparison
no contradiction (detailed summary) - wastes tokens, no help
optimal ordering of prompt - question first
Use paid models where appropriate, consider value of your time - don't bazooka a fly
Try different modalities, add picture/song to enrich narrative
